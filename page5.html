KABADDI

A physical team sport, kabaddi is. The goal of the game, which is played between two teams of seven players, is for a single offensive player, known as a "raider," to run into the other team's half of the court, touch out as many of their defenders as they can, and then return to their half of the court without being tackled by the defenders and all in the space of one breath.Players are removed from the game.
